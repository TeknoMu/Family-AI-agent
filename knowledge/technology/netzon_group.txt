NETZON GROUP - COMPANY OVERVIEW

Netzon Group is a technology and customer experience company founded in Stockholm, Sweden in 2012. The company started as a tech startup developing smart platforms, customized software, and digital solutions for startups and global brands. As clients grew, Netzon expanded into combining technology with human service, building tech-powered customer care operations.

GLOBAL OFFICES AND EXPANSION TIMELINE

2012: Founded in Stockholm, Sweden. Opened first office in the Philippines.
2023: Expanded to Singapore.
2025: Launched a hub in the Czech Republic to support European partners.

The company now delivers tech-powered customer care across industries and time zones, with a global workforce spanning Europe and Asia-Pacific.
